Privacy Policy

Smart Reminder & Event Planner

Effective date: March 2026

Smart Reminder & Event Planner respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information Collection

Smart Reminder & Event Planner is designed to help users create and manage reminders and events. The app may store reminder data locally on your device so you can use core features such as categories, priorities, icons, calendar view, and backup or restore functions.

Personal Data

We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information unless you choose to include it in reminder titles, notes, or backup content created by you.

Local Storage

Reminder information created inside the app may be stored locally on your device in order to provide app functionality.

Permissions

Depending on app features, permissions may be requested only when needed to support functionality such as notifications, backup, restore, or scheduled reminders.

Third-Party Services

If third-party services are added in future versions, such services may have their own privacy policies. Users should review them separately when applicable.

Data Security

We value your trust and aim to use reasonable measures to protect app data stored on the device. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed specifically to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may update this section with your support email before publishing the website.
